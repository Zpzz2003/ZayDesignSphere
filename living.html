Senior Living Options

Types of Retirement Homes

  • Independent Living Communities: Best for seniors who can live on their own but want access to assistance when needed.
  • Assisted Living Facilities: Great for seniors who need help with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, and medication management.
  • Nursing Homes: Provide medical care and supervision for seniors with serious health conditions.
  • Continuing Care Retirement Communities (CCRCs): Offer a combination of all levels of care, from independent living to nursing care, all in one location.

Choosing the right retirement home depends on your health, lifestyle preferences, and budget. It's important to tour several facilities and talk to residents before making a decision.
